This is Booker, my entry to the 2014 Clojure Cup competition.

How does it work?

  1. You enter a city and a date
  2. Magic
  3. It shows you some people who are going to that city around that date.

If nobody is found, you can make a profile and post your own plans for the next searcher.

Why do you need my facebook info?

We need facebook because it's easier than implementing our own login stuff, and so we can yank your name and profile picture without you having to provide them.
